# LOCKED Handwritten Image Style — Read This Before Generating ANY Image Prompt

> **Why this file exists:** Scribe kept re-deriving the style from memory and dropping pieces. This is the canonical block. Paste it into EVERY Gemini handwritten-image prompt. Do not rewrite from memory.

---

## RULE #0 — SPARSE, NOT DENSE (the most-violated rule — Dhruv's #1 feedback)
> Source: `memory/feedback_handwritten_flow.md`. Dhruv records 1.5-2 min videos using the notes as a BACKGROUND ANCHOR. The voiceover + the LinkedIn post carry the detail. The image does NOT.

- **3-4 flash cards / sticky notes MAX.** Never 5+.
- **Center flow 3 steps max.** Never 4-5 stacked boxes.
- **ONE strong visual idea**, not five competing zones.
- Skip the stats row unless the story is genuinely stat-heavy.
- No second/third margin stat-stacks. No bottom checklist AND a card stack AND margins. Pick ONE.
- Lots of whitespace.
- **Rule of thumb: if it takes more than 10 seconds to read all the text on the image, it's too dense — cut it in half.**

If a prompt has more than ~3 content zones, STOP and cut. Dense = rubbish. Sparse = right.

**GOLD-STANDARD REFERENCE = the "Claude Fable 5 is here" image (June 2026).** When in doubt, match THAT, not the dense "3 Prices" cost image (that one was too packed). What made Fable 5 right:
- LARGE, accurate portrait of Dhruv integrated on the page holding a marker (NOT a tiny corner bubble)
- Comparison data shown as **horizontal hand-drawn BAR CHARTS**, never text-stacks — bars are scannable in <10s
- Only **3 content blocks** + one bottom badge strip
- **ONE yellow highlight per row** (the winning number), not many
- A "SAVE THIS →" vertical tab on the right edge
- Big breathing room between blocks

## PALETTE (LOCKED — Rule 58)
- Black marker = text + lines
- Cyan **#18D1E0** = headers, underlines, connections, branding (NOT #00D4FF, NOT teal, NOT mint, NOT orange, NOT red)
- Yellow highlighter = ONLY hero numbers / key stats / the one phrase you want remembered
- Pastel sticky notes = yellow, light blue, pink, light green, light purple, grey (NO orange, NO red)

## THE PEN (locked — Dhruv's recurring miss)
**Brushed gun-metal SILVER STEEL pen with "AiwithDhruv" etched on the barrel in tiny cyan.** NEVER a blue plastic pen. NEVER a blue marker. The portrait holds it; a second one lies diagonally on the desk near bottom-left.

## AMBIENT / REALISM BLOCK (paste verbatim — this is what kept getting dropped)
```
=== AMBIENT / REALISM DETAILS (render ALL — this is what makes it real) ===
- A real BLACK COFFEE MUG on the desk at one paper edge, half on the page, soft shadow, dark coffee visible inside (optional faint steam wisp)
- A faint brown COFFEE RING STAIN near bottom-left, separate from the mug
- A black marker pen lying diagonally across the bottom, CAP OFF, cap resting separately nearby
- A blue ballpoint pen on the desk near the left margin
- 1-2 small metal paper clips on sticky-note edges
- 2-3 short strips of washi/masking tape on sticky-note corners
- Small doodle arrows, stars, and a tiny lightning bolt in empty spaces
- A couple of faint eraser smudges / crossed-out false-start scribbles in a margin
- Visible natural WOODEN DESK GRAIN at all 4 edges of the paper
- Spiral notebook binding rings along the LEFT edge
```

## STYLE — CRITICAL (paste verbatim)
```
- Real black marker on white lined notebook paper — authentic hand-drawn, NOT a clean digital/Canva design
- Slightly imperfect handwriting but always readable
- Cyan #18D1E0 for headers/branding ONLY
- Yellow highlighter ONLY for the hero number(s) + the one key phrase
- Pastel sticky notes at slight angles
- Portrait integrated as if sketched in the same sitting
- Everything hand-drawn — NO computer fonts
- Slight messiness = authenticity. Clean enough to read on a phone screen.
```
